Projects

Project PANOPTES is a citizen science project that aims to create a global network of robotics telescopes, which can be operated by citizen scientists and amatuer astronomers to observe a large fraction of the sky. I had the privilege to work with this project from August of 2019, and have learned a lot of things.

Currently I am primarily responsible for the maintenance of the telescope, which I pursue with a lot of rigor and perseverance.. I have been able to keep myself motivated throughout times of failure as technology is always unpredictable, the moment we fix one thing something else breaks down.


Panoptes Unit 18: Drukchen, The first PANOPTES unit in Bhutan and the 18th unit in the world. It is a robotic dslr imaging system used for tracking and observing the night sky.
